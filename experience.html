Work Experience

What I've accomplished over in my time here

My coding experience is of over 4 years, starting at the school level. Introductory courses to Java and C++ helped me gain a keen interest in programming and further develop these skills. I picked up web technologies like HTML, CSS and Javascript over the course of the past two years, whenever required for mini-projects and assignments.

Over the past two years (after joining IIT), I have coded mainly in Python and C++. Having an interest in algorithmic programming, I took part in Contests and have a four-star rating on CodeChef. I also have open source experience, having contributed to organisations like Mozilla, Plone, and Sympy. My team wound up 6th in our college at the Goldman Sachs Quant contest in 2017.

I am the deputy secretary of the Coding Club, IITG and have been in the club for the past year and half. I have been involved in projects involving chatbots, and also took part in Microsoft's Code.Fun.Do hackathon, where I created a price-tracking chatbot. I'm also the head of IITG MUN Web-ops, and I'm responsible for the maintenance of their website (IITGMUN) and a web-app used during the conference.
